How to Calculate VAT

Value Added Tax (VAT) is a consumption tax applied across the European Union. Businesses often need to add VAT to a price or remove VAT from a total amount.

Add VAT

Multiply the price by the VAT rate. Example: €100 with 20% VAT = €120.

Remove VAT

Divide the price by (1 + VAT rate). Example: €120 including VAT = €100 net.
